How It Works

Learn how Placement Analysis platform is helpful to Students and college admins during placements.

We offer you everything you need for a successful placement season; As a University or as a Student.

Create Student Accounts

The University/Colleges generate login credentials for every student. The students login to their accounts and complete their profile.

Get Notified

When a company is visiting for placements, the admins add the company details(Date, CTC, Eligibility). All the eligible students get notified on their provided contacts.

Generate Personalized Report

After every placement round, a personalized analysis is generated based on the student's performance.

Get Detailed Placement Analysis Report


After a company finishes its placement drive, a report having details of students who got placed & those who were rejected after any round is generated. This report is acessible to the admins only.


This helps the Admin to know about the weak points of every students & they can work with the faculty to help the student improve upon them.

Students Identify Their Weak Points


As a student, the user can view a history of the placement drives he has been a part of, and get an analysis of his/her performance in various rounds.


The students can also get to know their weak points from the admins & improve upon them in the skill enhancement activities organized by them.


It also helps in notifying the students about the companies which are visiting their campus & their required qualification to sit in the placements.
